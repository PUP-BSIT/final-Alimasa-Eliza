    Thoughts

    Online classes are really convenient. You don't have to wake up early or rush through your morning routine to make it to school on time. You can attend class from home or anywhere with internet, which saves time and removes the stress of commuting. But in face-to-face classes, like when learning programming, I find it easier to focus and learn. Direct interaction with teachers and classmates helps clear up questions quickly and lets us work together on problems. Plus, being in a structured classroom helps me stay focused without distractions. In online classes, distractions like chores and noisy neighbors can make it hard to concentrate. Many students also seem to be less engaged during online classes, doing other activities instead of paying attention. It can sometimes feel like background noise rather than effective learning.
